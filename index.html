Biography

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Stanford University. I received my PhD from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I interned twice at Qualcomm and more recently at Google where I designed privacy-aware unsupervised learning algorithms. I am the recipient of the 2015 ACM SIGMETRICS Best Paper Award, the 2012 Roberto Padovani Scholarship from Qualcomm's Research Center, and the 2016 Harold L. Olesen Award for Excellence in Undergraduate Teaching from UIUC. My research interests are interdisciplinary and span the areas of data and network sciences, privacy-preserving data analysis, machine learning, and information theory.
